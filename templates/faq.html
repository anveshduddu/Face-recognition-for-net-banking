FAQ's

What are types of fund transfers I can do?

You can do the following types of Fund Transfer: Fund Transfer between your own accounts Fund Transfer to any Other Express Bank account Instant Money Transfer

Is this facility available to Non-Resident Indians?

Yes, this facility is available to Non-Resident Indians also. Funds can be transferred from a NRE account to another NRE / NRO / Resident account. Similarly, funds can be transferred from a NRO account to another NRO / Resident account. However, funds transfer is not possible from resident accounts to NRE accounts.

Are there any charges for fund transfers?

Charges are applicable dependent of the type of transfer. You will see the charges applicable prior to submitting the fund transfer request.

Am I going to be charged for this security feature?

As of now, this service is free of charge and is a security measure for your online account safety.
